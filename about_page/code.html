My Story

I am a designer and technologist based in the intersection of aesthetics and utility. My journey began in the world of traditional print, where I learned the weight of a single pixel and the importance of white space.

Over the past six years, I have pivoted into the digital landscape, bringing a tactile sensibility to the screens we interact with daily. I believe that technology should not be loud; it should be an invisible facilitator of human connection. Whether I am architecting a complex IT infrastructure or fine-tuning a typography scale, my goal remains the same: intentionality.

AISEN was born out of a desire to create "digital heirlooms"—work that doesn't just survive the next trend cycle but matures alongside its users.
